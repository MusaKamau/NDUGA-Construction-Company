Our Services

Building/Construction

  • Our expertise in building and construction covers a wide range of projects, from residential homes to commercial spaces. With attention to detail and a focus on quality craftsmanship, we ensure that every structure we build stands strong and true.

Interior Finishing

  • Elevate your space with our finishing services. From gypsum ceiling installations to kitchen and wardrobe fittings, floor tile laying, and wood block or parquet flooring, we handle all aspects of interior and exterior finishing with precision and care.

Architectural Services

  • Entrust your vision to our skilled architects, who transform dreams into reality with innovative designs and meticulous planning tailored to your unique requirements.

Supply and Laying of Mahogany and Cedar Wood Blocks

  • Elevate your space with the timeless elegance of mahogany and cedar wood blocks sourced and expertly laid by Jenomill Designers Enterprises. Our premium selection of wood blocks adds warmth, sophistication, and durability to any interior or exterior surface, ensuring a lasting impression of luxury and craftsmanship.

Supply of Mahogany Door Frames and Shutters

  • Make a statement with the rich beauty of mahogany door frames and shutters supplied by Jenomill Designers Enterprises. Crafted with precision and attention to detail, our mahogany offerings enhance the aesthetics and functionality of any space, creating a welcoming and elegant atmosphere that reflects your unique style and sophistication.
